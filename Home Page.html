Bushfires

Some Bushfires are threatening to lives and homes but some are small enough with the right conditions won't be able to spread and cause evacuations among the residents that live near. With Climate Change making the heatwaves hotter and sun shining longer the chances of the threatening bushfires are evermore increasing.

What is a Bushfire?

Bushfires are essential to burning vegetation that is way too

long and is either an unplanned (natural)or a planned(Burnoff).

Bushfire Ratings

There are five differnet bushfire ratings from 'No Rating'

when no bushfires will be a threat to lives to 'Catastrophic'

which will be a threat to lives and homes.

What To Do?

If you live in a bushfire risk area, you always need a bushfire

plan just in case one comes your way and everyone in these

areas needs to be prepared.

The Main thing with bushfires is to be prepared so you don't end up trapped by the raging bushfire ahead.
